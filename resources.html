Transition to Econ 380

If you will be taking Econ 380 in the winter, then you will need to review the following concepts before the term begins:

  • Plotting and describing data
  • Sampling from a population
  • Sampling distributions (i.e. distributions of a statistic)
  • Hypothesis tests and confidence intervals for the mean
  • Simple linear regression
There are a number of online resources that can help you do this. I briefly outline a few options that I have vetted below.
